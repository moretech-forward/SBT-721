Auction Description
This contract implements a Dutch auction for an ERC721 token. The auction starts with a specified price and decreases over time based on a discount rate. Bidders can buy the token by sending enough Native(Eth, BNB, MATIC)/Stable currency(USDT, USDC, ...) before the auction expires.
